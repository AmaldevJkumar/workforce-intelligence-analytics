# Workforce Intelligence & Attrition Risk Platform

## Overview
This project simulates an enterprise-grade workforce analytics platform designed to help organisations monitor headcount trends, predict attrition risk, and support strategic workforce planning.

The platform combines analytics engineering, predictive analytics, and business intelligence to deliver actionable insights for HR and leadership teams.

---

## Business Objectives
- Monitor headcount and workforce distribution
- Identify attrition trends and early risk signals
- Support workforce planning and capacity management
- Enable data-driven HR decision-making
